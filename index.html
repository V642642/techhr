MORE THAN MEETS THE EYE AT TECHHR ’22

We are known to be tough on ourselves. Everything we’ve done in the past, is one difficult yardstick to surpass. Once
again TechHR 2022 looks at Work Tech with #FreshEyes and aims to get the best in the industry together to set trends
for the future. This is where new evolutions in business begin.
TechHR MASTERCLASS
As part of People Matters TechHR India 2022, we are privileged to launch BeNext Certification Masterclasses. These masterclasses are carefully curated by industry experts to help bridge the skill gaps that are critical not only for their own development but organisation's too.
View Masterclasses
IDEAS FOR FUTURE
What cutting-edge innovations have come into the market? Explore the latest and best tech products and solutions for your workplace challenges at the Ideas for the future exhibition. The uniquely laid out expo floor facilitates, accentuates and elevates business for you. Find that disruptive technology solution provider, a digital expert, your payroll answer or an AI enabling partner; and seed an innovative vision for your business.
UNCONFERENCE
Unconference is a space where we unlearn and learn in unique engaging formats and with an unexplored vision. Unconference hosts content in interesting forms like talk shows, quizzes, workshops, treasure hunts, movie learning sessions, robotic displays and much more. We 'll leave the rest as a pleasant surprise!
WOMEN IN BUSINESS
Empowered women empower women. Women in Tech is a unique offering at TechHR that will bring together women leaders to brainstorm on the future of HR technology and how to thrive in this changing world. You just can't miss it, but it's only for the Leading Ladies!
TechHR NIGHT FEST
Arguably, the only conference where business networking happens with cheers and laughter. Business does not stop at the conference, but is carried along to the TechHR Night Fest. Designed for the delegates, partners, and speakers, the evening would include the right environs to unwind and build great conversations.
Startup Brochure Apply
People Matters Startup Program is Asia’s largest confluence of Startups, VC, Investors, and Work Tech enthusiasts. The program aims at identifying the most progressive HR Technology startups in Asia.
If one is to calculate numbers from the last 7 years, it can be estimated that over 630+ startups have applied for the People Matters TechHR Startup program. This year‘s program offers attendees an opportunity to hear about the best innovations in the people and work tech space from the emerging startups of the India. The fact that some of the most renowned VCs have participated in the program is proof of how this platform will enable startups to be visible and will help them interact directly with investors.
